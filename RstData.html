The IPO offer documents (RHP and DRHP) are the key documents for an IPO. The DRHP (Draft Red Herring Prospectus) is the initial offer document filed with SEBI and upon approval, the RHP (Red Herring Prospectus) is filed. The IPO offer documents inform investors about the issuing company and provide details on the object of the issues, company financials, information on promoters, their shareholdings etc. The IPO Basis of Allotment is another important document released by the registrar of the IPO post allotment. It provides detail about the number of applications received during the IPO bidding process and how the allotment was done to each investor category (QIB, NII, and RII).
